Recurring Events

  • Hillman City Sway @ the Black and Tan Hall, Hillman City

    Join us once a month for Hillman City Sway! Presented in partnership with Black & Tan Hall and Blues Underground. Featuring a swanky evening of local live music, DJ's and dance instructors.


    Monthly on the fourth Thursday

    • Mixer and dance lessons 8-9pm
    • Band plays 9-11pm
    • All ages welcome!

  • Blues Underground ("BUG") @ Dance Underground in Capitol Hill

    Savoy's Blues Underground has been Seattle's mainstay social Blues dance night since 2005. As a branch of the Savoy Swing Club there have been many who have cared for Blues Underground as director, instructors, and DJs. As well, Blues Underground brings in world renowned instructors for workshops, host live music nights with local bands, and partners with local organizations supporting Seattle's Black community.


    Monthly on the third Friday

    • Dance lessons 8:30-9pm
    • DJ'd music from 9pm-12am
