Recurring Events

Check out these recurring events in the Seattle area! We're lucky to have amazing instructors, musicians, venues, and dancers here in the Seattle area who meet up to dance, learn, and share their love of Blues.


  • What The Blues - Lessons and Workshops @ the Lowdown Ballroom, Capitol Hill

    whattheblues.dance


    Lesson Series & Drop-Ins

    Our core weekly lessons timeslot is Tuesdays 6:30-8:30pm, at the Lowdown Ballroom. We will either have drop-in lessons, or 4- or 5-weeks series, depending on the month. Cancellations or special events occur occasionally, so make sure to check the calendar!


    Workshops

    Occasionally, we will teach workshops on more specific topics or styles. We also bring in out-of-town and national/international instructors to share their knowledge and experience with Blues.


    Private Lessons

    If you're after a more tailored learning experience, we also offer private lessons. Our current rates are $90 - $125 / hr, sliding scale. Please contact us to set up a time!

  • Hillman City Sway @ the Black and Tan Hall, Hillman City

    Join us once a month for Hillman City Sway! Presented in partnership with Black & Tan Hall and Blues Underground. Featuring a swanky evening of local live music, DJ's and dance instructors.

    blackandtanhall.com


    Monthly on the fourth Thursday

    • Mixer and dance lessons 8-9pm
    • Band plays 9-11pm
    • All ages welcome!
    • Instagram Facebook Location

  • Blues Underground ("BUG") @ Dance Underground in Capitol Hill

    Savoy's Blues Underground has been Seattle's mainstay social Blues dance night since 2005. As a branch of the Savoy Swing Club there have been many who have cared for Blues Underground as director, instructors, and DJs. As well, Blues Underground brings in world renowned instructors for workshops, host live music nights with local bands, and partners with local organizations supporting Seattle's Black community.


    Monthly on the third Friday

    • Dance lessons 8:30-9pm
    • DJ'd music from 9pm-12am
